# GymGrow

Auto-generated by ETS-AI 10x.

## Idea Summary

A SaaS product for small local gyms in India to generate and close leads from WhatsApp and Instagram.

## Problem Statement

Small local gyms in India struggle to efficiently capture leads from WhatsApp and Instagram DMs/forms, manage follow-ups, and track campaign performance, leading to lost potential customers.

## Target Persona

Indian small gym owner

## MVP Features

- Centralized inbox for WhatsApp and Instagram DMs (lead capture)
- Automated lead profile creation (name, contact, source, initial inquiry)
- Basic lead status tracking (e.g., New, Contacted, Follow-up, Converted, Lost)
- Ability to send direct WhatsApp and Instagram messages from the platform
- Automated follow-up reminders for leads
- Simple dashboard with key lead performance metrics (leads captured, converted)
- ML-driven lead scoring (High, Medium, Low conversion probability)

## Nice-to-have Features

- Customizable message templates for follow-ups
- Integration with other lead sources (e.g., website forms)
- Advanced analytics and reporting for marketing campaign performance
- Calendar integration for booking trial sessions/appointments
- Natural Language Processing (NLP) for advanced intent recognition from messages
- Automated content generation suggestions for marketing

## Core Modules

- Lead Ingestion & Integration (WhatsApp Business API, Instagram Graph API)
- Lead Management & CRM
- Communication Hub (Unified Messaging)
- ML Lead Scoring Engine
- Dashboard & Analytics
- Notification & Reminders

## Tech Stack

Python (Flask/FastAPI for backend, Scikit-learn for ML) + React.js (for a responsive frontend) + PostgreSQL (as the primary database) + Redis (for caching/real-time updates) + WhatsApp Business API & Instagram Graph API.

- Feasibility: yes
- Role in product: To prioritize leads for gym owners by predicting their likelihood of conversion, helping them focus on high-potential prospects and reduce lost opportunities from missed or delayed follow-ups.
